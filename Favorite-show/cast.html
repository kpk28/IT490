Cast Information

  • Tom Ellis as Lucifer Morningstar. Tom Ellis (born 17 November 1978 in Bangor, Gwynedd, North Wales, UK) is a Welsh actor best known for playing Dr. Oliver Cousins in the BBC Television soap opera EastEnders and Detective Sergeant Sam Speed in a Life on Mars parody on The Catherine Tate Show. He also has a twin sister and is married with three daughters. Before becoming a professional actor, Ellis played the French horn in the City of Sheffield Youth Orchestra.
  • Lauren German as Chloe Decker. Lauren German has Dutch ancestry as her grandfather, James German, was born in Amsterdam, Netherlands. He immigrated to the United States with his family as a child. Lauren German also has English ancestry from the other side of her family. She also almost became an anthropologist and made her first film debut in Down To You
  • Kevin Alejandro as Dan Espinoza was born on April 7, 1976 in San Antonio, Texas, USA as Kevin Michael Alejandro. He is an actor and producer, known for Arrow (2012), Southland (2009) and True Blood (2008). Since he was born he was very interested and deicated towards acting. He also started a scholarship with his friends in college to cover tution cost for underpriviledged theater student.
  • Lesley-Ann as Mazikeen. Lesley-Ann Brandt is an actress who is best known for her role as Maze in the TV series Lucifer. She was born in Cape Town, South Africa, but later moved to New Zealand at the age of 17, and then ultimately settled in the United States. She made her TV debut with the New Zealand soap opera known as Shortland Street, but actress rose to prominence with the character of Naevia on Spartacus: Blood and Sand
  • Rachel Harris as Linda Martin. Rachael Harris was born in Worthington, Ohio. She is a comedian and actress who played Tanya on the television series New Girl. She was nominated for an Independent Spirit Award for Best Female Lead for portraying Linda in the 2011 film Natural Selection.
